About AURA

What is AURA?

AURA is a revolutionary non-destructive gold purity analyzer that uses electromagnetic resonance technology to determine the purity of gold samples without causing any damage.

How it Works

Our advanced electromagnetic resonance method analyzes the unique frequency response of gold at different purity levels. The device measures resonance frequency, amplitude, and Q-factor to provide accurate purity readings.
